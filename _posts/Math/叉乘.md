# 视角 1：叉乘是矢量混合积的残片

$$f(\bm a, \bm b) \cdot \bm c = \begin{vmatrix}
    a_i & b_i & c_i \\
    a_j & b_j & c_j \\
    a_k & b_k & c_k \\
\end{vmatrix}$$

$$\bm a \times \bm b := f(\bm a, \bm b) = \begin{vmatrix}
    a_i & b_i & \bm i \\
    a_j & b_j & \bm j \\
    a_k & b_k & \bm k \\
\end{vmatrix}$$

$$\times(\bm a, \bm b, \bm c) = \begin{vmatrix}
    a_i & b_i & c_i & \bm i \\
    a_j & b_j & c_j & \bm j \\
    a_k & b_k & c_k & \bm k \\
    a_l & b_l & c_l & \bm l \\
\end{vmatrix}$$

# 视角 2：叉乘是线性变换

https://www.zhihu.com/question/524533468/answer/2410897303

令 $\bm r$ 到 $\bm v$ 的线性变换为 $\mathbf \Omega$

$$\bm v = \mathbf \Omega \bm r$$

$$\frac d {dt} \lvert \bm r \rvert^2 = \bm r \cdot \bm v = \bm r \cdot \mathbf \Omega \bm r = 0$$

二次型，没有对称部分，$\mathbf \Omega$ 只能反对称

$$\bm \omega \times \bm r = \mathbf \Omega \bm r$$

---

若 $\mathbf \Omega$ 不随时间变化

$$\bm r(t) = e^{\mathbf \Omega t} \bm r(0)$$

---

磁场是 $- \bm v$ 到 $\frac {\bm F} q$ 的线性变换

$$\frac {\bm F} q = - \mathbf B \bm v$$
