# Feynman 积分法

$$I = \int_a^b f(x) dx$$

$$F(t) = \int_a^b f(x,t) dx$$

$f(x) = f(x, t_0) \implies I = F(t_0)$

$$F'(t) = \int_a^b \frac \partial {\partial t} f(x, t) dx$$

$$F(t) = \int \int_a^b \frac \partial {\partial t} f(x, t) dx dt + C$$

## 倒数 & 调和数

$$\frac 1 k = \int_0^1 x^{k-1} dx$$

$$\sum_{k=1}^n \frac 1 k = \int_0^1 \frac {1 - t^n} {1 - t} dt$$

## Gamma & Beta 函数

$$\begin{aligned}
    \frac 1 {\binom n m} &= (n+1) B(m+1, n-m+1) \\
    &= (n+1) \int_0^1 x^m (1-x)^{n-m} dx
\end{aligned}$$

## Schwinger 参数化

$$\frac 1 {A^s} = \frac 1 {\Gamma(s)} \int_0^\infty x^{s-1} e^{-Ax} dx$$
